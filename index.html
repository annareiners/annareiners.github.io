Climbing - Safety First

Course Description

In rock climbing safety aspects have to be kept in mind at all times - for yourself, your climbing partner and all others around you.

This free online course introduces to important safety procedures in climbing such as tying the 8-knot accurately into the harness,
partner check and belaying with different devices.

It is designed for anyone who would like to get into rock climbing or check on advanced safety procedures while progressing in the sport.


Lesson I: Tying the 8-knot into your harness

Learning Outcomes

By the end of Lesson I the climbing beginner should be able to:
  • tie the 8-knot and the stopper knot
  • put on the harness
  • attach the 8-knot correctly to the harness

If you have a rope and harness handy, bring it close. If not, don't worry, you can also practise with any kind of cord long enough or just watch at first. Ready?
